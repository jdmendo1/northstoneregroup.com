What We Cover

From the basics to
the complex.

Our education content is built around what actually matters in real estate investing, not theory, but practical knowledge you can apply immediately. Topics are designed for both first-time investors and experienced operators looking to deepen their understanding.

Real Estate Fundamentals

How real estate investing works, key terminology, and how to evaluate an opportunity

Underwriting & Analysis

How to read a proforma, analyze cash flows, and evaluate investment returns

Financing & Capital

Understanding debt structures, loan types, and how capital is raised and structured

Asset Management

How to manage a property or portfolio to maximize performance and long-term value

Market Research

How to evaluate markets, identify opportunity, and understand supply and demand dynamics

Deal Structuring

How deals are put together, from acquisition through disposition, including partnerships and JVs
