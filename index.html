Who We Are

A firm built on
real experience.

Northstone Real Estate Group is a Pacific Northwest investment and operating company focused on multifamily and residential real estate. We partner with investors seeking disciplined, regionally focused exposure to one of the country's most resilient real estate markets and work directly with property owners who are ready to have an honest conversation about their options.

What sets Northstone apart isn't just what we invest in, it's how we operate. Every decision is grounded in institutional-grade analysis, transparent communication, and a long-term perspective. We don't chase deals. We build relationships, evaluate carefully, and execute with conviction.

Whether you're looking to deploy capital, explore a sale, or find a partner who understands the full lifecycle of a real estate investment, Northstone is built for that conversation.

Consulting Services

Whether you're looking to sharpen your investment strategy, build better financial infrastructure, or analyze your next deal, Northstone brings institutional-grade thinking to every engagement.

What We Offer

Built for operators, investors,
and anyone in between.

01

Business Analysis & Improvement

We evaluate your real estate business from the ground up, identifying gaps in operations, reporting, and strategy, and help you build the infrastructure to scale. Whether you're a growing operator or an established firm looking to level up, we deliver a clear-eyed assessment and a practical path forward.

02

Underwriting Model Development

A strong underwriting model is the foundation of every good investment decision. We build custom, institutional-grade models tailored to your asset type, strategy, and reporting needs, so you can evaluate deals faster and with more confidence.

03

Market Research & Analysis

Before you deploy capital, you need to understand the market. We provide in-depth research on target markets, submarkets, and competitive dynamics, giving you the context to make well-informed acquisition and investment decisions.

04

Deal Analysis & Due Diligence

From proformas and cash flow projections to debt structuring and return analysis, we work through the numbers with you on any deal, residential, multifamily, or commercial. Get a second set of experienced eyes before you commit.
